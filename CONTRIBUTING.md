### Architectural Overview

**Architectural Overview is a work in progress (last updated 02/18/2020)**

The front end framework uses [Angular](https://angular.dev/) and the backend is a variety of different scripts which pull the information from a variety of sources.

### How to contribute

1. Get environment up and running locally
1. Review issues on the local repos
   * [SDVV-Frontend](https://github.com/opensandiego/sdvv-frontend)
   * [SDVV-Backend](https://github.com/opensandiego/sdvv-backend)
1. Come to the meeting and discuss with project owner
